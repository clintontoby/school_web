ICT MEMBERS

Dr Abayomi Agbeyangi is the Head of the ICT department.The ICT coordinator, he is in charge overseeing the implementation and management of technology within the institution. His role involves tasks such as ensuring network security, budget management for technology expenses, developing and enforcing technology-related policies, fostering collaboration between departments, and contributing to long-term technology strategies. The ICT coordinator focuses on integrating technology into the curriculum and supporting teachers. Keeping abreast of technological trends and recommending updates or improvements is also a key aspect of this role.
