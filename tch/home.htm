About the ICT Unit

The ICT Unit, established during the 2015/2016 academic session, serves as a cornerstone of technological advancement at our university. Initially led by a single staff member, the unit has grown in tandem with the institution's evolving needs and aspirations.

In its infancy, the ICT Unit managed foundational elements such as the university website, internet access, and an internal telephone system (PABX). Recognizing the importance of keeping pace with technological advancements, the unit embarked on a transformative project in the 2017/2018 academic year: the development of a University Portal. This portal revolutionized crucial processes like admissions and result processing, offering efficiency and accessibility to students, faculty, and administrative staff alike. Through collaborative efforts with external consultants, the ICT Unit successfully implemented and has since maintained the portal through numerous upgrades, ensuring its continued relevance and functionality.

In 2019, leadership of the ICT Unit transitioned to a new director, ushering in a period of innovation and growth. Under successive leaderships, the unit has remained committed to its mission of providing reliable, innovative, and user-centric ICT solutions. Dr. Abayomi Agbeyangi, the current ICT Unit Coordinator, leads a dedicated team in supporting the university's technological infrastructure and services.

Today, the ICT Unit stands as a testament to our university's commitment to excellence and progress. With a focus on continuous improvement and staying abreast of emerging technologies, we strive to empower our university community with the tools and resources necessary to thrive in an increasingly digital world. As we look to the future, the ICT Unit remains dedicated to advancing our institution's mission of academic excellence, research innovation, and student success through the seamless integration of technology into every aspect of university life.

Mission Statement

Our mission at the ICT Unit is to empower Chrisland University with cutting-edge technology solutions that enhance teaching, learning, research, and administrative functions. We are committed to providing reliable, innovative, and user-centric ICT services that support the university's mission of academic excellence, research innovation, and student success.

Services Offered

Network Infrastructure Management: Ensuring the stability, security, and scalability of the university's network infrastructure. Software Development: Developing and maintaining the University software applications to meet the unique needs of the university community. Cybersecurity: Implementing robust security measures to protect university data and information assets from cyber threats and attacks. Technical Support: Providing timely assistance, troubleshooting, and resolution of ICT-related issues for students, faculty, and staff. Training Program: Offer training to enhance the ICT skills and knowledge of university stakeholders.

Achievement and Milestone

Continuous upgrades and enhancements to the University Portal to ensure its effectiveness and relevance. Contributions to technological innovation and excellence within the university community. Enhancement and support for the network infrastructure
